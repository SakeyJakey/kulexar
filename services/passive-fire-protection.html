/ Services / Passive Fire Protection

Audit Preparation Services

Our certificated experts will support with your third-party audit readiness for fire door installation, maintenance and fire stopping schemes. Services include workshops, training and desktop reviews.

Fire Door Surveys

Fire door surveys, carried out by our fully qualified certificated Fire Door Inspectors. Comprehensive assessment of the fire door and provision of a report detailing the inventory, condition and functionality of each door. We also offer periodic fire door inspection.

Fire Compartmentation Surveys

Fire compartmentation surveys: This survey involves a comprehensive review covering areas such as condition, efficacy, product use and compatibility, and provision of a fire compartment report including recommendations for rectification and improvement.

Passive Fire Stopping

We provice surveys that deliver a comprehensive assessment of the condition, effectiveness and compliance (Fire Safety Regulations, relevant third-party certifications and standards) of existing fire stopping. Following the survey you will receive a full report, including recommendation for repair or improvement. We offer passive fire stopping installation consultancy services to advise on the compatibility and suitability of proposed installation covering penetration seals, linear seals, cavity barriers, and air transfer grills.
